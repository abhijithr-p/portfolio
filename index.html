About Me

I am Abhijith R, a Bachelor of Engineering student specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning at T. John Institute of Technology (2023–2027). I am passionate about building AI-driven systems and smart technologies that solve real-world problems, particularly in areas such as healthcare, sustainability, smart mobility, and assistive technology. My work focuses on combining Artificial Intelligence, IoT, embedded systems, and data-driven design to create innovative solutions with real social impact. I have developed projects ranging from AI-based rehabilitation platforms and automated exam evaluation systems to smart agricultural and safety-focused technologies. My projects have been recognized at international hackathons and innovation expos, including being a finalist at the AgriSpark International Hackathon in Bangkok. I enjoy transforming ideas into functional prototypes and scalable systems, working across both software and hardware domains. I am particularly interested in exploring machine learning, computer vision, intelligent automation, and smart systems that can improve everyday life. Beyond engineering, I actively participate in sports such as football, basketball, and cricket, and I also have an interest in cinematography and visual storytelling, which helps me approach problem-solving with creativity and a broader perspective.
